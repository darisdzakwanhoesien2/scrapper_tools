About Tepma MEP Design

Tepma MEP Design d.o.o. is a mechanical and electrical engineering design office based in Belgrade, Serbia, specializing in BIM-based design and consulting for complex building projects in Germany and the EU. Our team of engineers develops complete technical building services (TGA/MEP) design — electrical, mechanical, and automation — for large-scale facilities such as hospitals, industrial plants, R&D centers, office complexes, and production facilities. We are part of an international engineering network and cooperate closely with German partners such as Salvia Elektrotechnik GmbH and Ingenieurbüro Lazić (Fulda). Our projects follow German standards (DIN, VDE, VDI, HOAI) and are executed in Revit, AutoCAD, and BIM 360 environments. Tepma MEP Design employs engineers with strong technical backgrounds and extensive field experience in oil & gas, shipbuilding, heavy steel fabrication, and building services design. The company culture promotes continuous learning, digital innovation, and international collaboration.

Role

Responsibilities

Main activities

Support the development and updating of BIM models for electrical and mechanical systems using Autodesk Revit.

Prepare and adjust technical drawings, schematics, and layouts in AutoCAD according to project requirements.

Assist senior engineers in quantity take-offs, cable and equipment lists, and system coordination.

Participate in internal design coordination meetings and learn about multidisciplinary workflows.

Conduct technical research on products, components, and standards relevant to MEP systems.

Help organize project documentation and data management within the BIM 360 platform.

Learn about German and EU design standards (DIN, VDE, VDI) and apply them under supervision.
